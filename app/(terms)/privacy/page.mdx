## CassetteOne Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-09-14

Weelone Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") operates the CassetteOne mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

### Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal data or information from you when you use the CassetteOne App. Our App is designed to respect your privacy and operate entirely on your device.

### Local Data Storage

CassetteOne stores data locally on your device to save your settings and preferences. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

### Data Security

As we do not collect or store any of your personal data on our servers, the risk of your data being compromised through our systems is minimized. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

### Your Consent

By using the CassetteOne App, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
